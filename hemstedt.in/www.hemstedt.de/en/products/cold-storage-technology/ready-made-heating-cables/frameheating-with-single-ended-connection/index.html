Door frame heating even with continuous soil

If cold storage room doors have no hump, so as sliding doors in cold storages that are driveable with forklifts or hand lift trucks Hemstedts single-ended frame heater is the ideal solution, since the heating cable must not be laid in a circle, thanks to the integrated return conductor. 

A copper braid protects the heating cable from damage.

Available versions:

Heating cable HEM-SiSiCu, with tinned copper braiding

with thermostat Order-No. 46500

without thermostat Order-No. 45500

Specifications frame heating for cold storage doors Order-No. 45500 / 46500

Heating cable HEM-SiSiCu, with tinned copper braiding
Protection class I
Connection single-end, with 1 PVC cold connection cable 3 x 0,75 mm²
Lenght of cold connection cable 0,50 m
Voltage 230 V, 50 Hz
Output in W/m 10, 15, 20, 25, 30 and 35 (the allowed load depends on the heat deduction conditions)
Availability:  
With thermostat Order-No. 46500: 0,5 - 16 m
Without thermostat Order-No. 45500: 0,50 – 20 m
Diameter 5,5, x 4,8 mm
Nominal limit temperature +135 °C
Note Different voltages, output and length are available on request. Extension of cold tail is possible for add. charge.
